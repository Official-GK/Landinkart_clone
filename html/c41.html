Frequently Asked Questions:

+

What is a Foreclosure Calculator?

A Foreclosure Calculator helps you calculate the savings and charges involved in foreclosing your loan before its original tenure. It helps you make an informed decision about early loan closure.

+

How is foreclosure amount calculated?

The foreclosure amount includes the remaining principal amount, any applicable foreclosure charges, and any other outstanding dues. The calculator helps you estimate this total amount.

+

What are foreclosure charges?

Foreclosure charges are fees levied by lenders when you close your loan before its original tenure. These charges typically range from 0-5% of the outstanding amount, depending on the lender and loan type.

+

When is the best time to foreclose a loan?

The best time to foreclose depends on your financial situation, the remaining loan amount, and the foreclosure charges. Generally, it's beneficial if you have surplus funds and the savings on interest outweigh the foreclosure charges.

+

What documents are needed for foreclosure?

You'll need your loan account statement, identity proof, and the foreclosure amount. Some lenders may also require a written application for foreclosure. Check with your lender for specific requirements.
